Why did you link to this page on Twitter?

I write blog posts on Substack, and share those posts on Twitter. After Elon Musk bought Twitter, he started suppressing tweets that contain links to Substack, including links that are shared with link shortners like Bitly. (This appears to have been accidentally left out of the "the algorithm.") To avoid having my tweets suppressed, I created this website so that I could share my posts without sharing links to Substack.

I apologize for costing you a click. On Twitter, freedom isn't free.
